You are a senior frontend developer and UX designer.

Design and build a **modern onboarding + landing webpage** for a platform called **SecureQuiz**.

---

### 🎯 Goal

When any user opens SecureQuiz:

* First show a **clean, engaging introduction**
* Explain:

  * What SecureQuiz is
  * Why it’s useful
  * How to use it
* Then guide them to:
  👉 **Create / Host a Quiz**

This platform is for **everyone** (students, teachers, creators, anyone), not just teachers.

---

### 🧩 Page Structure

#### 1. Hero Section (Top)

* Title:
  👉 “SecureQuiz – Smart & Secure Quiz Platform”
* Subtitle:
  👉 “Create, host, and attempt quizzes with built-in security and real-time results.”
* CTA Button:
  👉 “Create a Quiz” (primary)
  👉 “How it Works” (secondary)

---

#### 2. About Section

Explain clearly:

* What SecureQuiz does:

  * Create quizzes easily
  * Share with others
  * Track results
  * Prevent cheating (fullscreen tracking, tab switch detection)

Use simple language (not technical)

---

#### 3. Features Section (Cards/Grid)

Show features like:

* 🔒 Secure Attempt Mode
* 📊 Instant Results
* 📱 Works on Mobile & Desktop
* ⚡ Fast & Lightweight
* 🧠 Smart Tracking (tab switch, fullscreen exit)

---

#### 4. How It Works (Step-by-Step)

Make it super clear:

1. Enter your details
2. Add your quiz questions
3. Generate quiz link
4. Share with others
5. View results instantly

---

#### 5. Call to Action Section

* Big button:
  👉 “Start Creating Your Quiz”


#### 🎨 UI Design Guidelines

* Style: **Modern + Premium**
* Use:

  * Gradient backgrounds
  * Glassmorphism cards
  * Soft shadows
  * Rounded corners

* Add:

  * Smooth scroll animations
  * Hover effects
  * Subtle transitions

---

### 📱 Responsiveness

* Must work perfectly on:

  * Mobile
  * Tablet
  * Desktop

---

### ⚡ UX Requirements

* Fast loading
* Clear navigation
* No clutter
* Beginner-friendly explanation

---


### 💡 Goal

Make the first impression feel like:
👉 A **real SaaS product (like Quizizz/Kahoot)**
👉 Clean, professional, and easy to understand
👉 Any user should instantly know what to do next
